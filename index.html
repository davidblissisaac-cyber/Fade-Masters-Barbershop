The Craft

Built on chairs,
not corners cut.

Fade Masters opened in 2015 with two chairs, a single pair of clippers, and a simple rule: no guest leaves until the line is right. A decade later, that rule hasn't moved — even as the shop grew to three barbers and a waitlist.

We treat every cut as a craft, not a transaction. That means real consultations, hot towels that actually stay hot, and a shop that smells like leather and bay rum instead of a salon.
